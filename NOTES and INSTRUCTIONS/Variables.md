Types of Variables

1. **Nominal** - Categorical data with no inherent order or ranking
	ex- Gender, Color, Cuisine
- **Ordinal** - Categorical data with a meaningful order or ranking, but intervals between ranks are consistent or unknown.
	ex- Educatoin level, Customer satisfaction(Satisfied, Neutral, Unsatisfied)
- **Interval** - Numeric data with meaningful and consistent intervals between them without a true zero point.
	ex- Student marks with negative marking, Temp in Celsius or Fahrenheit
- **Ratio** - Numeric data where both intervals and ratios are meaningful and there is a true zero point.
	ex- Height, Weight, Income, Distance Travelled, Student marks
