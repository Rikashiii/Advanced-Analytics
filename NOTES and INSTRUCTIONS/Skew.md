##Skewness Formulae
- Pearson's First coefficient of Skewness (**Mode Skewness**):
---	
   	  Mean - Median
	-----------------
	Standard Deviation
---
- Another Variation:
---
	3 ( Mean - Median)
	------------------
	Standard Deviation
---
- Fisher-Pearson Coefficient of Skewness (**Moment Skewness):
---
	(Σ(xi-Mean)^3/n)
	----------------
	(Σ(xi-Mean)^2/n)^(3/2)
---
- Quantile-Based Skewness :
---
	(Q3+Q1 -2* Median)
	------------------
		(Q3 -Q1)
---
