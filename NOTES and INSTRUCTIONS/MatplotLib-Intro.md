#MatplotLib

Most popular plotting library in Python

- Seaborn and Pandas plots are built on top of Matplotlib
- Inspired by MATLAB-style plotting
- Supports Functional and OOP approaches

##Goals:
- Plot functional relationships
	ex. y=2x
- Plot raw data points (e.g., x= [1,2,3,4], y=[2,4,6,8])
---
##Types of Plots:
1. Line Plot - show trends and continuous data
- Scatter Plot- shows correlation bw two variables
- Bar Plot- compares categories
- Histogram- shows proportions or percentages
- Pie chart- shows proportions or percentages
- Heat map- captures correlation bw variables
- Box plot- Shows the distribution of a single variable in a box, useful for outliers
